Platform Doctor's Foundation

Platform is a dedicated non-profit organization comprising passionate Bangladeshi doctors and aspiring medical and dental students, united in their commitment to safeguarding the rights of healthcare professionals and providing invaluable support and guidance across various facets of their careers. Founded on September 26, 2013, Platform embodies a collective effort to foster a thriving healthcare ecosystem in Bangladesh by illuminating the bright facets, promising prospects, and abundant opportunities within the health sector.
